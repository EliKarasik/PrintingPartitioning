Object Partitioning for Support-Free 3D-Printing

This page provides supplementary material for our paper "Object Partitioning for Support-Free 3D-Printing".

Each object is rendered in two images showing:

  • The assembled model, with each printable subpart shown in unique color.
  • 3D Print layout, where the objects are separate and lay on their printing base plane.
The results are grouped according to the critical protrusion angle used for their partitioning. The objects are:
  • 135: Airplane | Bearing | Cat | Deer | Dolphin | Fertility | Genus | Helix | Inuksuk | Octopus | Sculpture | Tree
  • 150: Armadillo | Fertility | Isidore Horse | Sculpture | Gargoyle
